----The Quiet Town----

Backrooms Level "?"

Classification: B1 - Moderately Hazardous

Entities: Smiler, The Thing, Mimic, The Frown.

Description: Entities roam the streets at night, they will eat anyone in their sight. Gather items at day time and stay indoors at night to avoid getting seen by the entities.

Exits: N1: If you get lucky, you might no-clip out of this level by entering a random door.
N2: If you enter a house that's coloured red and purple you will find a hatch inside that leads to what seems like an endless drop, jump down and you'll no-clip out of this level.
N3: There is an entity that looks similar to The Smiler but has a frown instead of a smile on its face, its called "The Frown", if you're able to kill it, all humans in the level will no-clip out, beware though, the entity is rare and far more dangerous than Smiler.
